Soda Makers


One reusable sparkling water maker can save up to 1282 single use plastic bottles from our planet per year
Refills


Buying refills of products like washing detergents, coffee, hand wash and use refillable drinking bottles; it's usually cheaper and it helps recuce package waste.
Plastic Free


Plastic free shopping: Leeds has a plastic free store called The Jar Tree situated in the Kirkgate Market, in which you can use your own containers to reduce plastic waste.
